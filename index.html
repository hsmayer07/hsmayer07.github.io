BoilerTime Bio

Founded out of a CS307--Software Engineering project at Purdue, BoilerTime is a schedule optimization algorithm for undergraduate student class schedules at Purdue University. Created by a team of six engineers, BoilerTime is driven by a proprietary optimization algorithm that uses user selected parameters such as time of day, professor rating, and course outcomes to select the perfect courses--and class times.

  • Website: boilerti.me
  • Public Beta Release: Spring 2023
  • Anticipated General Release: Winter 2023/Summer 2024
